Our Mission

Roots & Wings is built with the vision of preserving, digitizing, and celebrating India's incredibly diverse and rich cultural heritage. From the towering peaks of Buddhist stupas in Bihar to the intricate carvings of Dravidian temples in Tamil Nadu, our mission is to make history accessible to everyone, everywhere, using cutting-edge WebXR and AI technologies.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to download an app to use AR?

No! Roots & Wings operates entirely in your browser using WebXR. As long as you have a modern smartphone with a compatible browser like Chrome or Safari, you can project monuments instantly.

How many languages are supported?

The platform supports over 10 major Indian languages including Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, and Bengali. Our native voice engine can both understand your commands and read out descriptions flawlessly.

Is this platform free to use?

Absolutely. Roots & Wings is an open-access platform dedicated to digitizing and providing free educational access to Indian cultural heritage for everyone.
